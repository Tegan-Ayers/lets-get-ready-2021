Free Response Analysis

The survey include two free response questions:

  1. Please tell us any concerns you have about college as a result of the coronavirus.
  2. What was the most helpful area of support or advice you received from your coach and/or LGR?

Responses to each of these questions was analyzed in order to extract themes/topics. Two analyses are presented below: word clouds and topic modeling. A summary of the analysis is at the bottom of the page.

Select which Program (Transition, Success), Group (Students, Coaches) and Question (Covid Impact, LGR Resources) you'd like to view using the toggle buttons. Word clouds and topics plots will automatically populate with the correct data.

Topic Modeling

Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) was used to extract "topics" from the free text responses. This technique assumes that each free response is a mixture of topics, and the presence of each word is attributed to a specific topic.
