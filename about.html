Survey Overview

Transition and Success Core+ students and coaches were surveyed between May 2020 and January 2021. Survey questions focused on two primary topics:

  1. What LGR resources were most impactful during the 2019-2020 and 2020-2021 school years?
  2. How were students' college goals and experiences impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic?

Data Snapshot

The number of students and coaches surveyed is outlined in the table below. The bulk of the analysis is focused on student responses.

Group Total
Transition Students 421
Success Students 630

Success Coaches 71

All data was anonymized and aggregated. No individual responses are shown.
