Survey Overview

Transition and Success Core+ students and coaches were surveyed between May 2020 and January 2021. The survey focused on two specific questions:

  1. What LGR resources were most impactful during the 2019-2020 and 2020-2021 school years?
  2. How were students' college goals and experiences impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic?

All data was anonymized and aggregated. No individual responses are shown.
