Topic Modeling

Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) was used to extract "topics" from the free text responses. This technique assumes that each free response is a mixture of topics, and the presence of each word is attributed to a specific topic.

The most salient words are plotted below. To view the words associated with a specific "topic", select a button.
